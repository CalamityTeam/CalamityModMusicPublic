[h1]Calamity Mod Music[/h1]
[url=https://discord.gg/calamity]Discord[/url] [url=https://calamitymod.wiki.gg]Official Wiki[/url]

[b]This mod adds the Calamity soundtrack and music boxes for each song on the soundtrack.[/b] It is intended to be used alongside the Calamity Mod, but can be used alone. You will need to cheat in the music boxes if you do not use the Calamity Mod.

For Calamity game content, e.g. items and bosses, subscribe to the main mod:
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2824688072
